Krannert And Purdude

Regarded as one of the nation’s elite business schools, Purdue’s Krannert School of Management (http://www.krannert.purdue.edu/home.php) is integrated with one of the world’s leading engineering, science and technology universities and teaches business concepts in a STEM context. Krannert uniquely approaches business learning from a research-based, quantitative angle that develops students’ analytical skills and, naturally, produces graduates with the resourceful, think-fast problem-solving abilities every modern-day commercial enterprise demands. Krannert’s graduates are immediately prepared to contribute, and have the leadership skills to quickly move up the ladder.
