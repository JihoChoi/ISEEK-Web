Global Engagement


In the summer of 2016, Purdue and 39 other universities around the country implemented the Mandela Washington Fellowship through the Young African Leaders Initiative (YALI) https://yali.state.gov, created in 2014 by President Obama and funded by the US Department of State. Young leaders from all over Africa came to campus for 6 weeks to learn and network, and eventually brought their knowledge back to Africa. Purdue hosted 25 entrepreneurs and led a multidisciplinary program that included lectures, field visits, and networking events on entrepreneurship, engineering, and agriculture. ISEEK served as the Krannert representative. In addition to attending the planning meetings through the spring semester and then getting to know the Fellows over the course of various sessions from June to July, ISEEK coordinated the following activities specific to Krannert:
